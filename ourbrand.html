The Upperlink brand is our Masterbrand, evolving from the founding
vision of the company, to stimulate appetite for interaction and
connect the different key elements within the digital and financial
ecosystems.

Our visual identity and brand expressions have remained as constant
as the SUN and the red “Arrow Head Shield” which is the integral part
of the wordmark encapsulates our progressive drive towards pushing
the boundaries of creativity , traversing new frontiers and bringing
Solutions that will transform lives .Hence our slogan,” Transforming
Lives with Technology.“

Our mandate is to create digital and financial Solutions that will be
used by at least one person in every family of Five in Africa

The philosophy behind our BAMS brand is Transparency. The
all white colour of the wordmark on a blue background
depicts Transparency. The World Bank is encouraging Nigeria
and her States to embrace transparency under the
programme called States Fiscal Transparency, Accountability
and Sustainability.(SFTAS) which is well funded by the bank.

BAMS is a strategic tool in Public Sector transformation and is
therefore being embraced by all the State Governments of
the federation. Our mandate is to develop home grown
Solution to solve a Nigerian problem and make our country
less dependent on foreign Software.

The BAMS brand embodies maturity in solving age long
problems of Transparency and Accountability in Public
Spending.

Paychoice is positioned as a convenient platform providing
digital and financial services to everyone, anytime, anyhow
and from the comfort of their homes.The spirit of
Transforming Lives through Technology has made us develop
a Solution that will be the right choice always.

The red ” tick mark” stimulates appetite to always go for it
when considering which Solution to use for payments,
collections and even Payroll .
